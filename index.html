Why Civilytix Matters

Civilytix ensures transparency, accountability, and optimization in infrastructure projects. Historical mismanagement of projects has led to cost overruns, delays, and even disasters. Civilytix provides solutions to prevent these issues by leveraging cutting-edge technology.

Preventing Project Failures

Many projects face delays and cost overruns due to improper management. For example, the Big Dig in Boston was originally estimated at $2.8 billion but ended up costing over $14 billion due to poor planning and management.

Reducing Financial Waste

According to the World Bank, nearly 30% of global infrastructure project costs are wasted due to corruption and inefficiencies. Corruption scandals, such as the Petrobras scandal in Brazil, demonstrate the importance of monitoring bids and finances properly.

Enhancing Transparency and Accountability

Lack of audits and maintenance leads to infrastructure failures. For example, the Genoa Bridge Collapse in 2018 resulted in the loss of 43 lives, largely due to insufficient audits and maintenance.

Improving Tender Selection

Selecting the wrong contractor through improper tendering processes can lead to disastrous outcomes. The Berlin Brandenburg Airport was delayed for almost a decade and exceeded its budget by billions due to mismanagement and wrong contractor selection.

Key Features of Civilytix

  • Project Prediction: Predict project costs based on materials, labor, and other factors.
  • Bids Ranking: Rank bids using MCDM methods and detect anomalies.
  • Financial Monitoring: Track financial transactions and ensure funds are properly utilized.
  • Audit Scheduling: Schedule audits and download reports to ensure project quality and compliance.
